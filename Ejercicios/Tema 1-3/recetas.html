Salmorejo

A perfect dish for the summer

List of ingredients (3 persons):

  • 12 Tomatoes
  • 3 Eggs
  • 3 Medium potatoes (optional)
  • Bread crumbs
  • 4 sp Olive oil
  • 2 tsp Salt
  • 3 Garlic cloves
  • Jamón serrano
  • 3 Cans of tuna (optional)

Steps:

  1. Peel the tomatoes and remove the seeds. Cut the tomatoes into small pieces and triturate them with a food shreder until you get a smooth mixture.
  2. Cut the garlic into small pieces and add it to the tomato mixture. Add the bread crumbs, olive oil and salt and shred it all again.
  3. Add more olive oil or more bread crumbs depending on the texture you'd like and keep mixing it. Once you get the right texture, put it into the fridge.
  4. Peel and cut the potatoes into small squares. Deep fry them.
  5. Boil the eggs.
  6. Once you've finished boiling the eggs and frying the potatoes, serve the tomato mixture into a bowl and accompany it with the eggs, fried potatoes, tuna and jamón serrano.

Lemon cake

For the sweet lovers

List of ingredients:

  • 2 Lemon yogurts
  • 3 Measures of floor
  • 2 Measures of sugar
  • 1 Measure of olive oil
  • 1 Lemon peeling
  • 1 sachet of baking powder

Steps:

  1. Empty the lemon yogurts into a big bowl.
  2. Use the yogurt containers to measure the floor and add it to the bowl. Do the same with the olive oil and sugar.
  3. Peel the lemon and add the peeling to the bowl in shreded small pieces.
  4. Mix it all together and add the baking powder until it's smooth.
  5. Preheat the oven at 180ºC.
  6. Grease up a pyrex container.
  7. Add the mixture to the pyrex container and put it into the oven for 45 min at 180ºC

Beef steak

Easy meat recipe

List of ingredients:

  • 300-500 grams beef steak
  • 1 sp Olive oil
  • 1 sp of butter
  • Coarse salt
  • Black pepper

Steps:

  1. Add the olive oil and butter to a frying pan and heat it at max power.
  2. Once the butter is melted add the steak and fry it between 2 and 3 minutes on each side.
  3. Put the steak in a plate and salt-pepper it to taste.
